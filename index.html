Tips for Owning a Legendary Muscle Car

Buying a muscle car is a journey into automotive greatness. Here’s how to choose your 2025 legend:

  • Performance Goals: Prioritize track-ready power (e.g., Camaro ZL1) or daily-driven style (e.g., Mustang GT).
  • Budget Planning: Prices range from $40,000 to $120,000. Factor in maintenance, fuel, and insurance.
  • Test Drives: Experience the roar and handling of multiple models to find your match.
  • Resale Value: Icons like the Mustang and Corvette hold strong value. Research market trends.
  • Modern Features: Look for advanced infotainment, safety systems, and connectivity.
